How we price junk removal in Caloundra

Our pricing is designed to be fair and transparent. You pay a service fee that covers our team, labour and truck, and then tip fees are passed on at cost — whatever the waste facility charges us is exactly what you pay, with zero markup. We'll give you a realistic estimate of tip costs upfront based on what we're removing, so you're never caught off guard.

For a typical Caloundra garage cleanout or single-room clear, the whole job can often be wrapped up in under two hours. For larger deceased estate jobs or full property clear-outs, we'll scope it properly and give you a clear picture before starting.
