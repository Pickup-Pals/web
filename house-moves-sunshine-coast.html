Full house and unit moves

Moving house on the Sunshine Coast, done right

Moving is one of the most stressful things you can do. There's a lot that can go wrong — removalists who don't show up, vague quotes that blow out on the day, teams that rush and damage your belongings. Pickup Pals exists to be the opposite of that.

We cover full house and unit moves across the entire Sunshine Coast — from Caloundra in the south to Noosa in the north, and all suburbs in between. Whether you're moving from a two-bedroom unit in Mooloolaba or a four-bedroom house in Buderim, we'll quote you a fixed upfront price, show up on time and get it done carefully.

How the quote works

Every house move starts with an upfront quote. We base this on the size of the property, the volume and nature of your belongings, the distance between addresses and any access considerations at either end — things like stairs, lifts, long carry distances or narrow driveways.

You'll receive a confirmed price before we start, not a vague hourly estimate that could balloon unpredictably. If on move day the job is tracking over the estimated time — perhaps there's more to move than we accounted for, or access is more complex than described — we stop and speak to you before any extra charges apply. We'll explain the situation, give you the overtime rate and ask your approval to continue. You will never receive a surprise bill at the end.

Transparency on overtime: The overtime rate is explained to you at the time of quote, before you book. There are no hidden clauses and no surprises. If the job finishes on time or early, you pay the quoted price — not a cent more.

Apartments, townhouses and units

Multi-storey living is common across the Sunshine Coast, and we're well set up for it. We handle apartment and unit moves with lifts, stairwells, tight car parks and body corporate access requirements as a routine part of our work. If your building has specific requirements — dedicated lift bookings, time restrictions or loading dock rules — let us know when you enquire and we'll plan around them.

Townhouses with internal stairs, narrow driveways and courtyard access? No problem. We come prepared with the right gear and the right team size for your specific property.

What to expect on move day

  • We confirm your booking the day before to make sure everything is on track.
  • The team arrives at the agreed time with the truck loaded and ready to work.
  • We do a quick walkthrough of both properties with you to confirm the plan.
  • We load everything carefully — furniture wrapped and protected as needed.
  • Everything is placed in the correct rooms at the new address, not left in a pile.
  • We do a final check with you before we wrap up. Payment is only taken once you're happy.

Local and intrastate moves

Most of our house moves are local — between Sunshine Coast suburbs. But if you're moving from the Sunshine Coast to Brisbane, the Gold Coast or elsewhere in Queensland, we can quote on that too. Call us to discuss and we'll work out a price that makes sense for the distance involved.

Not doing a full house move? Have a look at our furniture removalist service for single items or partial moves, or our junk removal service if you need to clear things out before or after moving.
