How pricing works for Noosa junk removal

We keep pricing transparent and fair across all Noosa jobs. You pay a service fee covering our team, labour and truck, and then tip fees are passed on at cost — exactly what the waste facility charges us, not a dollar more.

Tip fee estimates are given upfront before we start, so you have a clear picture of what you'll pay. For larger jobs — full property clear-outs, multi-room estate clearances, or significant renovation waste — we'll do a proper scope and give you a detailed quote before committing.
