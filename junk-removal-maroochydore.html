Clearing Maroochydore, one load at a time

Maroochydore is the Sunshine Coast's commercial and civic heart — and with that comes a steady stream of junk that needs to go somewhere. Whether you're a homeowner in a Cotton Tree unit doing a long-overdue cleanout, a business clearing an office in the CBD, or a tradie finishing a renovation in Mountain Creek, Pickup Pals is the call to make.

We operate throughout the full Maroochydore region: the CBD, Cotton Tree, Mooloolaba, Alexandra Headland, Kuluin, Kunda Park, Mountain Creek and Bokarina. If you're not sure whether we service your street, just call — we almost certainly do.

Common jobs we do in Maroochydore

Unit and apartment cleanouts

Maroochydore and Cotton Tree have a high concentration of units and apartments, and end-of-lease cleanouts are one of our most common jobs in the area. We're comfortable with lifts, stairwells and tight parking situations. We'll clear everything from old furniture and appliances to bags of general rubbish, and we leave the place ready for your property manager's inspection.

Commercial office clearances

Businesses relocating or closing in Maroochydore's CBD or the Kunda Park and Kuluin industrial precincts need a fast, reliable crew. We handle office furniture, filing cabinets, electronic equipment, partitioning and general fitout rubbish. We can work outside business hours if you need the space cleared without disrupting your team.

Furniture disposal

Sofas, beds, wardrobes, dining sets — big pieces that won't fit in a skip bin or a regular vehicle. We load it, haul it and sort through it before anything goes to the tip. If a piece is still in decent shape, we'll look at donating it locally before adding it to the tip load.

Renovation rubbish

Tiles, timber, plasterboard, old bathroom fixtures — renovation waste is heavy, awkward and often requires the right disposal stream. We haul it all and sort recyclable materials out of the mix where we can.

A genuine point of difference on pricing: Most junk removal operators build a margin into their tip fees. We don't. You pay a service fee for our time and labour, and then the exact transfer station charge — nothing more. We show you the tip receipt if you want to see it. That's the Pickup Pals way.
