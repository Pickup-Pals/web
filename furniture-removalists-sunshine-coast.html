Furniture moves, big and small

What we move — and how it works

Not every move is a full house. Sometimes you've scored a great sofa on Facebook Marketplace and need help getting it up two flights of stairs. Sometimes you're rearranging a bedroom and the wardrobe is simply too heavy to shift on your own. Sometimes you're moving into a new place and just need the bulky stuff handled professionally.

That's exactly what Pickup Pals is here for. We're furniture removalists on the Sunshine Coast who don't have a minimum job size. One piece, a full room, or a whole house — the same reliable team shows up, does the heavy lifting and puts everything exactly where you want it.

What we move

Single item moves

Sofas, beds, dining tables, fridges, washing machines, pianos, large bookshelves — if it's too big or too heavy for a standard vehicle, we've got it. Single-item jobs are quoted upfront and are often bookable with short notice, including same-day when availability allows.

Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree pickups

This is one of the most common calls we get. You've found exactly what you were looking for online, you've sorted the deal with the seller — now you need someone to collect it and deliver it to your door. We do Marketplace and Gumtree pickups across the Sunshine Coast constantly. Give us the pickup and drop-off addresses, tell us what the item is, and we'll sort an upfront price. You don't even need to be present at the pickup if the seller can handle access.

Full room moves

Moving a bedroom, a lounge room or a study? We'll load everything carefully, transport it to the new address and reassemble or place items in the right rooms. No need for a full house move booking — we can handle individual rooms as standalone jobs.

Stair access and tight spaces

A lot of furniture removalists on the Sunshine Coast charge extra for stairs or refuse awkward access points. We don't. Stairwells, narrow hallways, tight corners and lifts are just part of the job. If access at your property is genuinely complex, let us know when you call so we can send the right team size and gear — but we will not refuse the job based on access.

Furniture moves vs. full house moves: A furniture move is any job where we're moving individual pieces or specific rooms, usually from one property to another or within the same property. A full house move covers all your belongings in one go. Both are quoted upfront, but they're priced differently — a furniture move tends to be simpler and faster to quote. Not sure which applies? Just call us and we'll help you work it out.

How it works

  • Call 0434 411 372 or fill in the quote form — tell us what you're moving, where from, where to.
  • We call you back with a confirmed, upfront price. No vague estimates.
  • Our team shows up at the agreed time, ready to work.
  • We load, transport and place everything exactly where you need it.
  • You pay once you're happy — card or cash, no payment before completion.

If the job ends up taking longer than quoted, we'll stop, explain why and get your agreement before any additional charges apply. That's the Pickup Pals promise. Have a look at our full house moves page if you're moving an entire home.
