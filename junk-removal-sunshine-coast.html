Sunshine Coast's straightforward junk removal service

Getting rid of a pile of junk shouldn't be complicated, and it definitely shouldn't come with hidden costs. At Pickup Pals, we keep things simple: you pay a service fee to cover our team, labour and truck, and any tip fees are passed on to you exactly as the facility charges us — not a dollar more.

We service the entire Sunshine Coast, from Caloundra and Kawana Waters in the south through to Maroochydore, Buderim and Nambour in the middle, all the way up to Noosa, Tewantin and Cooroy in the north. One call and we come to you.

What we remove

Whether it's one bulky item or a whole property's worth of rubbish, we've got the truck and the team for it. Common jobs we handle every week include:

  • Old furniture — sofas, beds, wardrobes, dining sets, mattresses
  • Whitegoods and appliances — fridges, washing machines, dryers, dishwashers
  • Green waste — palm fronds, branches, garden clippings, soil bags
  • General household rubbish and hard waste
  • Office equipment — desks, chairs, filing cabinets, e-waste
  • Garage and shed cleanouts — tools, shelving, miscellaneous junk
  • Deceased estate clearouts — we handle these with care and respect
  • Renovation debris — timber, tiles, plasterboard, light fitout waste

The only things we cannot take are hazardous materials: asbestos, chemicals, paint, gas bottles and medical waste. If you're unsure whether something qualifies, just give us a call on 0434 411 372 and we'll let you know straight away.

How junk removal pricing works

We believe you should know exactly what you're paying before we show up. Our pricing has two components:

  • Service fee — covers our time, labour, transport and the truck
  • Tip fees — the actual facility charges, passed on at cost with zero markup

Because tip fees vary depending on the weight and type of material, we'll estimate these upfront as accurately as we can and confirm the final amount once we're at the facility. There are no nasty surprises.

Eco-conscious disposal

We don't just take everything to the tip and leave it at that. Before disposal, we sort through your load and set aside anything usable for donation to local charities and op shops. Metals, electronics and cardboard are sent to the appropriate recycling facilities. Only what genuinely cannot be diverted from landfill makes it to general waste. It's the right way to do it, and it keeps costs lower too.
